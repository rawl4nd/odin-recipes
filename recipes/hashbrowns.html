Homemade Crispy HashBrowns

Good, old-fashioned, restaurant-style hash browns

Ingredients

  • 2 medium russet potatoes, shredded
  • 1/2 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup oil for frying, or as needed
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients
  2. Rinse shredded potatoes until water is clear, then drain and squeeze dry.
  3. Place shreds in a bowl and mix in the onion, flour, and egg until evenly distributed.
  4. Heat about 1/4 inch of oil in a large heave skillet over medium-high heat. When oil is sizzling hot, place potatoes into the pan in a 1/2 inch thick layer. Cover the whole bottom of the pan, or make sparate piles like pancakes.
  5. Cook until nicely browned on the bottom, then flip over and brown on the other side. It should take at least 5 minutes per side. If you are cooking them in one big piece, it can be cut into quarters.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
